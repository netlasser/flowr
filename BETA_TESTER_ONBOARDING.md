# FLOWR Beta Tester Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the FLOWR beta! We're excited to have you as a tester. This guide will help you get started and make the most of your testing experience.

---

## Getting Started

### 1. Access the Beta
- Visit: [https://beta.flowr.app](https://beta.flowr.app) (placeholder URL)
- Sign in using the email address you provided in your beta application
- If you have trouble accessing the app, please reach out in the `#support` channel on Discord

### 2. Explore the Core Features
FLOWR's beta includes these main features:
- **ZoneBoard**: Organize tasks into cognitive zones
- **FlowGuardian**: Focus timer to minimize context switching
- **TransitionBuffer**: Mandatory breaks between zones
- **WhiplashAnalytics**: Dashboard to track your context switching habits

## How to Provide Feedback

We value your feedback! Here are the ways you can share it:

### 1. In-App Feedback Form
- Click the floating feedback button in the bottom-right corner
- Choose a feedback type (Bug, Feature, General)
- Write your message and submit

### 2. Discord Server
Join our dedicated beta Discord server: [Invite Link]
- `#announcements`: Official updates from the FLOWR team
- `#general`: Chat with other beta testers
- `#bug-reports`: Report issues you encounter
- `#feature-requests`: Suggest new features or improvements

### 3. Direct Contact
If you need to reach us directly:
- Email: beta@flowr.app
- Discord DM: @flowr-team

## What to Test

We'd love your feedback on these areas:

### Core Functionality
- Does ZoneBoard work as expected?
- Is FlowGuardian helpful for staying focused?
- Does TransitionBuffer feel effective?
- Are the WhiplashAnalytics useful?

### Usability
- Is the app easy to navigate?
- Are the UI elements clear and intuitive?
- Did you encounter any confusing parts?

### Performance
- Does the app load quickly?
- Are there any laggy or unresponsive parts?
- Did you experience any crashes?
